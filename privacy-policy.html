This Privacy Policy explains how the المصمم المحترف كتابة على الصور (“Pro Designer — Write on Photos”, “the App”, “we”, “us”), published by CONPLY, handles information when you use it. The App works primarily on your device: your photos are edited locally and are not uploaded to our servers.

1 Information We Process

The App does not require you to create an account, and we do not ask for your name, email, or phone number to use the App. The information involved falls into these categories:

Information you provide / create

  • Photos and designs you select, edit, and export — processed locally on your device.
  • App preferences (theme, language, notification settings) — stored locally on your device.

Information collected automatically by third-party SDKs

  • Device & technical data (device model, OS version, language, app version, coarse region, and identifiers such as the advertising ID) may be collected by Google services we integrate (advertising, app stability, and configuration). See Third-Party Services.

2 Your Photos & Media

When you pick an image, apply text, frames, stickers, filters, makeup, or background removal, all of that editing happens on your device. We do not upload, store, or share your photos on our servers, and we do not use your photos to train any model.

Edited images are saved only where you choose to save them (your device gallery) or shared through the apps you explicitly select via the system share sheet.

3 On-Device Features (Face Detection & AI Tools)

Features such as makeup/beautification and face-based tools use Google ML Kit face detection that runs entirely on your device. Your photos are not sent to Google or to us for these features. Background removal and filters are likewise processed locally.

4 How We Use Information

  • To provide and operate the App’s editing features.
  • To remember your settings and preferences on your device.
  • To display and measure advertising (where enabled) and keep ads relevant and limited.
  • To maintain stability and improve performance.
  • To deliver optional notifications you have enabled.

5 Third-Party Services

We rely on the following Google services. Their processing is governed by Google’s own policies:

  • Google AdMob (advertising) — How Google uses ads data.
  • Google Play Services / Google Mobile Ads SDK — core advertising and device services.
  • Firebase Remote Config — remotely configures app settings (for example, enabling ads and this policy’s link). It does not collect your photos.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging — used only if you enable notifications, to deliver them.

Google’s Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy.

6 Advertising & Consent

The App may show ads provided by Google AdMob. To support ads, the Google Mobile Ads SDK may use a device advertising identifier.

If you are in the European Economic Area, the UK, or Switzerland, we present a consent message using Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) before personalized ads are shown. You can review or change your choices at any time from the App’s Settings → Manage ad preferences (shown where applicable).

You can limit ad personalization on your device at any time via Android Settings → Privacy → Ads, including resetting or deleting your advertising ID.

7 Device Permissions

  • Photos / Media / Storage — to let you pick images and save your edited results.
  • Camera (optional) — only if you choose to take a photo inside the App.
  • Notifications (optional) — only if you enable reminders/notifications.
  • Internet / Network — to load ads and fetch remote configuration.

You can grant or revoke these permissions anytime in your Android system settings.

8 Data Security

Because your content stays on your device, it benefits from your device’s own security. For data handled by Google services, transmission uses industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we and our providers take reasonable measures to protect data.

9 Data Retention

We do not retain your photos or designs on any server. App preferences remain on your device until you clear the App’s data or uninstall it. Data processed by third-party services is retained according to their policies.

10 Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

11 Your Rights

Depending on your location (including the EU/EEA under GDPR and California under CCPA/CPRA), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data, and to object to or opt out of personalized advertising.

  • Manage ad consent in the App (Settings) or via your device’s ad settings.
  • Uninstall the App or clear its data to remove locally stored preferences.
  • Contact us to exercise applicable data rights regarding third-party processing.

12 International Data Transfers

Our third-party providers (such as Google) may process data on servers located in countries other than yours. Where required, such transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards under those providers’ policies.

13 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date above. Significant changes may be highlighted within the App or on this page.
